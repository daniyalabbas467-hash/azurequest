🎟️ Exam Vouchers — pay half for the real exam

Microsoft runs free online events called Virtual Training Days. Attend one → you get a 50% discount on the matching certification exam. No catch — here's the whole thing without the confusion.
1️⃣
Register with the RIGHT emailSign up for the event with the exact same email as your Microsoft Learn profile. Mismatched emails are the #1 reason people never get the discount. No Learn profile yet? Create one first, then register.
2️⃣
Attend the full eventOnline, free, usually two half-days (~3–4 hours each). Camera off, no test at the end — but you must attend all sessions to qualify.
3️⃣
Wait 5–10 daysThe discount gets attached to your Microsoft Learn profile. Don't hunt for a code in your inbox — it usually just appears automatically on the payment page when you schedule the exam.
4️⃣
Schedule within 90 daysExam page on Microsoft Learn → “Schedule exam” → the 50% off shows at checkout. You must schedule within 90 days of the event — book early, the exam date itself can be later.

⚠️ The 4 ways people lose the voucher

📧
Different emailsEvent registered with one email, Learn profile on another → discount never shows. Fix: use one email everywhere.
⏭️
Skipping sessionsAttendance is tracked. Join every session, even if you just listen in the background.
⏰
Missing the 90-day windowThe discount expires 90 days after the event. Schedule the exam as soon as it appears.
🧾
Waiting for a voucher codeThere often is no code — the discount applies automatically at checkout (same email!). Not there after 10 days? Contact Microsoft certification support.
